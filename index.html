ABHINAY

If stage is your happy place and showmanship your passion then 'Abhinay' is the destination. Abhinay is for performers who embrace the stage to bring alive myriads of emotions with an impact so strong that the audience is left awestruck and longing for more. Abhinay has also been an effective medium to bring social causes in the centre-light and starting conversations.

BANDISH

Indian music never fails to draw us like a moth to its flame and so does Bandish. Bringing all the colours of Indian music together, Bandish comprises of solo singing, duets, band performances and original compositions. It's a perfect platform for blooming young buds in the music world to showcase their talents and get recognition at an embellished stage as Bandish.

CROSSWINDS

Crosswindz is one of the country's oldest band battle. It's all about the ripping rock band clash. Here the best bands play their hearts out and compete for the whacking title of 'the best band'. The crowd is left captivated by enthralling performances by various colleges.

NATRAJ

From the elegance of classical dance to the zeal of hip-hop, from the spellbinding contemporaries to the breathtaking street dances, Natraj is a whirlwind tour of some of the best enthralling dance performances by various colleges giving fresh talent a chance to take the centre stage.

MIRAGE

Mirage, is the fashion and lifestyle event organised by Kashiyatra, the annual socio-cultural fest of IIT-BHU.
The presence of acclaimed designers and highly talented and charismatic models makes Mirage an extremely celebrated event. Every year the most creative heads come out with some out of the box designs and get a chance to showcase their talent at a platform as big as Mirage. Elements inspired from Indian culture and tradition have always been the part of the show and keep our culture alive, blending it with a modern tinge. What makes this event more classy is the presence of top notches from the fashion industry.

ENQUIZTA

The bright minds driven by curiosity lock heads in various events of Enquizta to prove their prowess in a world full of knowledge. For all those who think they are the know-it-all, Enquizta is the perfect competition to meet and compete with the like-minded geeks.
